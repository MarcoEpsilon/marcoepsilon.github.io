2017 英语(一)

Directions:

You are to write a email to James Cook, a newly arrived Austrlian professor,recommending some tourist attractions in your city. Please give reasons for your recommendation.

Self Analysis:

1. 第一段表达出对James Cook教授的到来感到高兴

2. 第二段开始介绍自己城市的景点(这里就用北京), 向教授介绍故宫,说明这是古代帝王居住的场所,历史意味深重,非常值得游玩,然后介绍万里长城,摆出谚语"不到长城非好汉",说明万里长城是多么的宏伟壮阔,古代人民智慧和劳动的结晶

3. 第三段表达出希望教授能够玩的开心

Writing:

Dear James Cook:

I am looking forward to your coming here. Before your coming, please allow me to introduce some beautiful tourist place for you.

Beijing,a city with strong history and culture.First, i suggest that you can visit Imperial Palace, it is a place which emperor ever lived. It is worth visiting,since it have deeply history scene. Otherwise, a proverb goes, "One who fails to reach the Great Wall is not a hero". So it is a good choice to reach the Great wall,when you arrive it, your will find how magnificent the Great Wall is.

Finally, i hope that you can have fun at BeiJing.

Yours truly,

Li Ming

2005 英语

Directions:

Two months ago you got a job as an editor for the magazine Design & Fashions. But now you find that the work is not what you expected. You decide to quit. Write a letter to your boss, Mr. Wang,telling him your decision, stating your reason(s), and making an apology.

Self Analysis:

1. 说明自己来信的目的,并表示在这个岗位收获颇多(能力的提升,认识到很多朋友)

2. 转折表达自己的兴趣不在这个岗位上,表示出自己目前有了对未来的规划,想要离开岗位,去拼搏

3. 为自己的突然离职感到抱歉,表示自己希望老板能够找到比自己更好的员工接手这份工作

Writing:(Offical Answer)

Dear Mr.wang

I am greatful to have been employed for the past two months as an editor for your magazine Design & Fashions. I appreciate the opportunity of having worked here with you and my other colleagues. The experiences I had here are ones I will not forget for my entire life.

However, as a young man whose primary interest is in computer science rather than fashion design, I have found that my present job doesn't fall in with my previous training and strength. I have therefore decide quit this job to pursue other opportunities that may conform better to my educational background.

Please accept my sincere apologies for any inconveniences my leaving may cause.

Yours truly,

Li Ming
